About Piezoelectric Roads

Piezoelectric roads use special crystals embedded under the road surface. When vehicles pass over, mechanical pressure is converted into electricity. This clean energy can power streetlights, traffic signals, or even nearby towns. EcoWatt helps you simulate how much electricity could be generated based on traffic and road conditions.
